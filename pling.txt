An open-source dot-cursor theme, inspired by Adder Inds. Original "Google_Cursor" by ful1e5.

Check [url=https://github.com/fsubmersible-technologies/Adder_Cursor]README.md[/url] for installation, uninstallation, personalize cursor sizes or colors.

[b]XCursor Sizes:[/b]
22x22, 24x24, 28x28, 32x32, 40x40, 48x48, 56x56, 64x64, 72x72, 80x80, 88x88, 96x96

[b]Windows Cursor Size:[/b]
- 16x16 - Small
- 24x24 - Regular
- 32x32 - Large
- 48x48 - Extra Large
